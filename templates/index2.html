Welcome, this Motivation Management tool.
You can take a look at your motivation by graph show your motivation and F_value.
What is F_value? This is a motivation index calculated in Expectancy Theory by V.H.Vloom.
Motivation index parameter has Valence, Instrumentary, Expectancy.
This can be calculated by the following formula.

F = E *(ΣVI)(F’>0)
F: motivation index E: expectancy(-1.0~1.0) V: valence(-1.0~1.0) I: instrumentary(-1.0~1.0)

Expectancy which you expect to succeed at your activities, valence which show probability that activity induces another activity, instrumentary which show your effort will connect your next step are three parameter to caluculate motivation index(F_value).

Please compare your motivation you thought and this value, and utilize your management of your motivation!
